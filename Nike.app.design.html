Project Concept

The project started as a personal initiative after my classes. Passionate about the Nike brand and its aesthetic, I wanted to practice web design by redefining the mobile shopping experience. As a daily consumer of their products, my passion for the brand motivated me to create something visually true to its identity.

Research and Analysis

Before diving into the actual design, I conducted extensive research. I explored a lot of inspiration through Figma Community, Behance, and Awwwards to gather ideas for the project. At that time, I wasn't very focused on UX/UI design because I didn’t yet have the necessary skills. Instead, I focused primarily on creating pages that I felt were important for the mobile site, without paying much attention to mobile site structure or user experience as a whole.
